Two Column with Header & Footer

Ver 1.0

There is a main outer div which centers the page and is a fixed width. There are 4 main div's within this outer div, header (#hdr), bar (#bar), body (#body) and footer (#ftr). The body div holds 2 more divs, the left div which is for navigation and the right div which is for our content.
